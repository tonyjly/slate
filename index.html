About Me

I am passionate about pharmacy and healthcare technology.

My early studies began at the University of Washington where I earned my Bachelor of Science in Microbiology. Undergraduate research in the Department of Pharmaceutics helped further mature my analytical mind, while simultaneously working in a community pharmacy helped me realize my true calling.

I spent the next four years on the East Coast where I earned my Doctor of Pharmacy degree from the University of New England in 2016.

I am interested in a career that enables me to provide compassionate, patient-centered health care to patients. Technology, innovation, and constant improvement all drive me to do what I do. I am interested in the practice of Kaizen (改善), which is a philosophy based on constant improvement. While striving for excellence is the plan, the ultimate goal is to provide the best care for my patients and their families.

I am currently looking for a pharmacist position. Although I am based in Western Washington, I am willing to relocate.
